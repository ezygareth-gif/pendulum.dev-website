Land Development

From raw land acquisition to fully serviced stands, we handle the entire pipeline — surveying, zoning, servicing, and subdivision. We turn underutilised land into thriving residential and commercial opportunities across Zimbabwe.

🏡
02

Real Estate Management

Buying, selling, or leasing? Our real estate arm manages residential, commercial, and industrial properties with precision. We deliver maximum returns for property owners and seamless transactions for buyers.

Engineering Services

Civil, structural, and mechanical engineering solutions executed to international standards. From concept and design to site supervision and completion — our engineers build with excellence.
